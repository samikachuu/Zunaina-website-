About AkiyaFinder

AkiyaFinder is dedicated to solving Japan's abandoned house (akiya) crisis by connecting buyers from around the world with vacant properties in rural and urban Japan. We offer a seamless search experience and support system to help revitalize these forgotten homes.

Why Choose Us?

Interactive Map Search

Explore abandoned properties using our integrated map system, filtered by region, price, and renovation status.

Verified Listings

We verify properties with local government sources to ensure reliability and legal clarity.

Smart Recommendations

Using AI, we suggest akiya listings that match your budget, interests, and renovation goals.

Global Buyer Support

We offer bilingual support for international buyers interested in owning a home in Japan.

Restoration Resources

Connect with local architects, contractors, and renovation experts to bring your akiya back to life.
